You are about to enter the Catacombs: a dusty boneyard of deprecated java applets, broken links, and html 1.0 upon which this site was first built, back in the dying days of the last century. If you're a rifters completist you could find stuff of interest here: text and illustrations to enhance the Lenie Clarke experience, much as the newer wings set the stage for Blindsight and Echopraxia. But none of it has been updated in years— and that which isn't outright broken is, at best, quaint.

You have been warned.
